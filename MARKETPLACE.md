## Facebook Marketplace Alerts — Sedan Hunt

### Schedule
- Daily at **5:00 PM Toronto time**.
- Send **best available matches** (not forced to exactly 15).

### Current Search Rules
- Price: **CA$2,500–CA$6,000**
- Body type: **Sedan only**
- Model year: **2010 or newer**
- Mileage: **Under 200,000 km**
- Condition: no accidents, clean title, no rebuilt/salvage
- Exclude: damaged or “as-is” listings
- Preference: automatic transmission, clean interior/exterior, no warning lights, safety-passable, reliable brands prioritized
- Region: Ontario first, nearby provinces acceptable

### Duplicate Policy
- Do not resend the same listing URL/ID.
- Track seen listings in: `memory/marketplace-sedan-seen.json`

### Notes
- If fewer listings meet quality filters on a given day, send fewer.
- Never store account passwords in memory files.
